Tips

  • Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information like names or dates.
  • Consider using a passphrase, a combination of random words.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Change your passwords regularly, especially for important accounts.

These tips can significantly enhance the security of your passwords.
